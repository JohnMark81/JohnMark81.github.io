My Learning Experience

What have I learned from the experience? (ICT Technical Training)

I'm so thankful for the experiences I had during my OJT. They all helped me learn a lot of things, including how to act professionally when encountering different situations in the area. I gained perspective on the life of a programmer or IT-related work, and I was able to fulfill my role effectively as a leader of our team.

My experiences with the people around me on-site/online.

Being with the people around me during my OJT made me realize that we all may have different roles in the company, but each position is important for smoother work. I was glad they were always ready to teach us, just as we were always ready to learn. I also learned that the kinder and more polite you are when interacting with your co-workers, the more peaceful and productive the environment becomes.

The most memorable event during my practicums

That time when we made a significant mistake as interns is the most memorable experience for me. It was a critical situation as we nearly faced the possibility of being dismissed from the company we were working for. However, that experience taught us a valuable lesson on the importance of being cautious and adhering to the rules and regulations of any future companies we may work for. It served as a reminder of the need for diligence and accountability in our professional endeavors.

What I can recommend for the improvement of the Practicum Program

I believe that in order to maximize learning opportunities as IT students, it is important to be given the chance to experience different companies. One suggestion is to facilitate exchanges with other groups, allowing us to switch after two months of our initial OJT placement and gain exposure to different work environments. This would provide valuable insights and broaden our understanding of the industry.

My advice to those who will take their practicum soon.

My advice is to enjoy every day of your life as an intern. Be very observant and always take the initiative to complete important tasks for the company. Apply everything you have learned, as you will be representing our University. Aim high, STI!
